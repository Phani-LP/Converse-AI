Welcome to Django Chatbot

Powered by Gemini 1.5 Flash, a lightweight, fast, and efficient AI model designed for high-volume, high-frequency tasks.

Features

  • Context Window: Up to 1 million tokens, allowing it to process large amounts of data.
  • Use Cases: Summarization, chat, image and video captioning, and data extraction.
  • Cost: Cheaper than Gemini 1.5 Pro.
  • Speed: The fastest Gemini model served in the API.
  • Multimodal Reasoning: Capable of reasoning across large amounts of information.

How It's Used

  • Available in public preview in Google AI Studio and Vertex AI.
  • Highly capable of handling complex, large-scale AI tasks.
  • Optimized for high-volume, high-frequency tasks at scale.
